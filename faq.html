Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Paintatronics™?

Paintatronics™ is a puzzle game that combines creativity and challenge, allowing players to complete pixel art puzzles and reveal colorful images. Each puzzle is part of an album, and completing them unlocks fun facts about the depicted entity—whether it's a dinosaur, animal, food, or other fascinating topics—making the experience both engaging and educational.

How do I play Paintatronics™?

In Paintatronics™, your goal is to complete intricate pixel art puzzles by filling in the correct colors in each square. As you progress, the puzzles become more challenging and detailed, rewarding you with beautifully completed pictures. Just tap and drag to paint, and don’t worry – there are hints to help if you get stuck!

Data Privacy

Does Paintatronics™ collect personal data?

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as full name, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise locations is collected. Paintatronics™ only collects minimal, anonymous data, such as gameplay progress, to enhance the game experience. All data is aggregated and anonymized through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the game.

How is my gameplay data used?

Gameplay data helps us improve the game by understanding popular features and albums. This data is never shared or sold to third parties and is used solely for internal development and analysis.

Do you store my information for any marketing purposes?

We do not use player data for marketing. Paintatronics™ is focused solely on enhancing your gaming experience.

Technical Support

Which devices are supported?

Paintatronics™ is optimized for iPhone, iPad, and Android devices. The app supports iOS 13 and above, as well as Android 5.1 Lollipop and above. For the best experience, ensure your device software is up-to-date.

I'm experiencing a bug in the game. How can I report it?

Sorry to hear that! You can report any bugs or issues through the in-game by emailing support@paintatronics.com. Providing screenshots or steps to reproduce the issue helps us address it faster.

Can I play Paintatronics™ offline?

Yes, Paintatronics™ is fully playable offline. You can enjoy puzzles anytime without needing an internet connection.
